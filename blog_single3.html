Benang Karya Community: Creating Together, Growing Together

Benang Karya Community is more than just a gathering place for crochet lovers; it's a place to grow, share and nurture creativity together. Here, members of all skill levels can join crochet classes, pattern discussions, and inspiration sharing sessions. ‘Gathering together in a warm and supportive atmosphere provides a space for each member to exchange knowledge and new ideas' said one of the community members.

In addition to regular activities, the community is also involved in social projects such as knitting for charity. Through this programme, members crochet with a spirit of mutual cooperation to help others. This social project not only creates a close bond among the members, but also adds meaning to each knit. ‘Every knit we knit brings warmth not only to the recipient, but also to us who made it together.

‘Kreasi Bareng Benang’ a crochet session with a different theme each month, is one of our favourite activities. This monthly challenge encourages members to try new patterns and techniques, while providing an opportunity to step out of their comfort zone. The resulting work is often showcased on social media, which gives each participant public appreciation. ‘Each challenge gives me the urge to grow and get new inspiration’ says one of the programme's participants.

Here, we not only create, but also grow and become part of something bigger.

The community also shares inspiring stories from its members, such as those who have successfully developed their knitting hobby into a business. These stories provide tips and motivation for starting a business, managing time, and marketing products. With a variety of activities and support, Benang Karya is not just a community, but a platform that strengthens and inspires its members.
