About us

Benang Karya is a business that grew out of a deep love for crochet and handicrafts. Established with the aim of introducing and preserving the art of crochet to the community, Benang Karya exists as a place where everyone can express their creativity. We believe that every knitted piece has a unique value and story, and we are proud to help our customers create meaningful knitted products, whether as gifts, accessories, and more.

As a custom crochet specialist, Benang Karya provides custom crochet services. We understand that everyone has different tastes and needs, which is why we offer flexible design options to create truly personalised products. From bags, hats, scarves, to home décor, every custom order is meticulously crafted, using high-quality yarns and crochet techniques mastered by our team of experts. The result is knitted pieces that are not only beautiful, but also durable and functional.

In addition to customised services, Benang Karya also organises workshops for those who want to learn or explore the art of crochet. Our workshops are designed for various levels, from beginners who are just learning the basics of crochet to advanced classes for more complex techniques. Guided by experienced instructors, our workshops offer a fun and interactive learning experience. We provide all the equipment needed, so participants can focus on their creative process while building new crochet skills.

More than just a business, Benang Karya is a community for crochet lovers. We want to create a warm and supportive space where crochet enthusiasts from all walks of life can meet, share experiences and inspire each other. Through regular meetings, discussions, and collaborative activities, the Benang Karya community provides a platform to share ideas, projects, and support in each member's creative journey. We believe that by working together, we can create bigger and more meaningful works.

With the spirit to keep creating and innovating, Benang Karya is committed to providing the best service for our customers and community members. We hope to be a friend to anyone who wants to know more about the world of crochet or simply enjoy the beauty of quality knitted products. Together with Benang Karya, let's create something that is not only beautiful to look at, but also heartwarming.
