About Benang Karya

Benang Karya is a business that grew out of a deep love for crochet and handicrafts. Established with the aim of introducing and preserving the art of crochet to the community, Benang Karya exists as a place where everyone can express their creativity. We believe that every knitted piece has a unique value and story, and we are proud to help our customers create meaningful knitted products, whether as gifts, accessories, and more.
