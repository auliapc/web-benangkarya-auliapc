Fun Crocheting to Bond the Benang Karya Community

The crocheting events organised by Benang Karya are always looked forward to as a moment to relax and get together. Held in cafes or parks, the relaxed and cosy atmosphere makes every member feel welcome. Participants, both beginners and experienced, are welcomed with coffee or tea and crochet kits. ‘This event gives me time to enjoy my hobby, while strengthening friendships’ said a member.

After getting to know each other, participants began working on their individual crochet projects. Community mentors are also present to help and guide participants who encounter difficulties. ‘Sharing experiences and learning from those who are more experienced makes this event so valuable’ said a beginner.

One of the fun activities that we always look forward to is small competitions, such as the ‘speed crochet’ competition where participants compete to make simple patterns in a limited amount of time. This activity adds excitement and laughter among participants. In addition, we often do projects together. These collaborative projects strengthen the bond between members as they work together. ‘Crocheting together for a good cause makes the results feel more meaningful,’ said a participant.

crocheting together for a good cause makes the results feel more meaningful

Towards the end of the event, participants showed off their finished and work-in-progress pieces to each other. The moment was full of praise and encouragement, giving inspiration and confidence to all. This knitting event left a lasting impression and helped the Benang Karya community continue to grow, learn, and expand friendships through a shared love of crochet.
